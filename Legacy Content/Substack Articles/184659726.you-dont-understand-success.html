Jake Paul is not a good person. Look at that photo. Maybe he’s happy, maybe he’s fulfilled, maybe he’s reached the pinnacle of what he believes success looks like.

But maybe he’s also an asshole who took time out of his day, no, no that’s not accurate, who paid someone probably minimum wage to lay out money and golden guns in a (likely) rented set that looks like a private jet.
