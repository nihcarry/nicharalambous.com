This is a somewhat strange deviation from my usual solo builder content but it’s been a tough year for me and my canine companions.

When I was 28 years old Mumford arrived in my life. He was the cutest puppy anybody has ever seen. I’m not exaggerating. If you don’t believe me, just take a look:

For 13 years he’s been with my partner and I for every adventure, house move, country move, continent move, hike, road trip, lazy Sundays and everything else we’ve done. When he Mumford was 1 we decided he needed a buddy and then Mango entered our lives and we were four.

About a year ago Mango unexpectedly passed away riddled with cancer. That was a tough blow. One day she was fine and the next she was gone, literally. It all unfolded so quickly. This left Mumford and my partner and I. A few months after Mumford had been alone without Mango started to notice that he was restless at night. I initially thought it was because he was alone for the first time in over a decade. Then his breathing started to feel a little more laboured than it should. He’s old so some strain is to be expected. Then he started to, kind of, wander around and pause in odd places; up against a random wall, on the corner of a chair, near the fridge. Never doing anything other than standing.

I started to notice that he was becoming clingy and attached to both of us as we moved through the house normally.
