Ten Nicisms To Help You Live A Happier Life

There is nothing after this. 

This is your one shot at life. 

You don’t get a do-over. 

You don’t get a mulligan. 

Grab it with both fucking hands and squeeze every drop out of this life. 


Learn the rules but ignore them. 

The rules were created by other people, just like you. 

You need to understand them but to live an exceptional life you have to ignore them and forge your own path. 

Be honest if possible. 

Honesty is not always the best policy. Sometimes lying to the one you love to protect them is OK. 

Be more. 

Every single day you can try to be more than you are today. 

Be nicer, be meaner, be kinder, be happier, be sadder, be fulfiled or empty. 

It’s OK to be anything as long as you are trying to be more every day. 

Be patient.

Waiting is an action. 

It is perfectly OK to take a break, read a book and think about life while your plans take hold and grow a life of their own. 

Waiting is an action. 

Trust people until they give you a reason not to. 

I don’t believe that respect and trust need to be earned. 

I like to respect and trust people upfront. If they give me a reason not to respect or trust them later on, then it’s over. No backsies. 

Listen more, talk less. 

You know what you have to say because you thought it. 

Listen to what others have to say. Hear their thoughts before you respond. 

Everything is easier without ego.

Ego is the enemy.

The less you need to pump up your ego, the more successful you will become. 

Help others grow and succeed and your success will only expand. 

Strong opinions loosely held.

It’s OK to change your opinion if the facts change. 

You do not need to be the same person you were at 19 or 29 or 45. You can change, grow and rebuild who you are as the facts around your life change. 

Don’t be a tree. 

Burn it all down if you have to. 

Sometimes walking away is the only sane thing to do. 

That relationship, business, partner, house, guitar or memory that you are holding on to for dear life… burn it. 

It’s OK to burn it down, walk away and start again. 

BONUS: Ask more questions. 

The key to a more full life is curiosity. 

You need to ask more questions about your life, your choices and your existence. 

Why do you do things the way that you do them? Are you choosing this existence or just existing in the world until you die?

Ask. More. Questions.
