No, you can’t pick my brain

Find a better way to ask people for a favour

Seeking out advice from others is a great way to learn quickly from people who have experience that you need. 

I have asked for advice a lot in the past and still seek out smart people often to ask for help. I have never used the phrase “pick your brain”. 

I hate that phrase and I hate the way it’s used. 

People spend a long time gaining knowledge, experience, and skill. When you pop them an email with the title: “Can I pick your brain?” it comes across as dismissive and leaves them in a very awkward position.
