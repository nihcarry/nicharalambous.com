You Succeed Because You Fail

So many people are afraid of failure, but here’s the thing;

You succeed because you fail.

Not in spite of your failures.

I have never met a successful person who avoids failure.

The trick is to mitigate catastrophic failure.

How?

- Small tests.

- Frequent tests.

- Test one thing at a time.

- Understand that it isn’t a failure if you have learned something, it’s a lesson.

- Iterate the next experiment from your previous learnings.

- Shorten build and launch times.

- Decouple your self worth from the outcome.

Remember, failing does not make you a failure.
