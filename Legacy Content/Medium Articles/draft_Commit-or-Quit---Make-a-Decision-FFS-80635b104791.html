Commit or Quit — Make a Decision FFS

Most people have too many different points of view about business and life

Any decision is better than a lack of decision. If you don’t believe that then think about the last time you and your partner tried to choose which take-out you felt like for Saturday night dinner. 

I am baffled by people have given no thought to why they do what they do. These people trundle along blissfully ignorant that they are living someone else’s life and choices. 

These are the people who can’t commit to anything specific or don’t know why they’ve committed to something at all. They are religious by default, they are liberal because their parents were, or they support a team because they were born near that particular team. 

It’s these people who hold strong opinions strongly and never change their minds. I prefer to hold strong opinions weakly. If the facts change, I know how to reassess my opinion and change my point of view. This is a superpower in an ever-changing world. We are rapidly learning new things about the universe, physics, quantum physics, nature, life and one another. If you hold the same opinion today than you did 20 years ago without having taken a good, hard look at why you believe what you believe, then you’re in for trouble. 

I’ve seen too many businesses tank because the founder or leader couldn’t commit to a path and pivoted themselves into oblivion.
