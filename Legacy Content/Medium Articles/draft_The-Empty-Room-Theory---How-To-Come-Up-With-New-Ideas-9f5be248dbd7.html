The Empty Room Theory — How To Come Up With New Ideas

Coming up with ideas and solutions cannot happen in isolation

I often talk to people who are trying to come up with a new idea or a solution to a problem but are stuck and seeking some way out. 

The first thing I ask is what they are doing when they are thinking about their new idea. More often than not people tell me that they are sitting at the desk and googling things. 

This is fine as a start but ultimately you don’t stumble on ideas while you google things. The main reason this is true is because you are googling things you are already thinking about and know of. You aren’t experiencing anything fresh, new or different from what you’re used to.
