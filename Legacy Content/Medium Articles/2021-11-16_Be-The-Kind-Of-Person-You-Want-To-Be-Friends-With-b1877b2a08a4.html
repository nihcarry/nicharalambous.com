Be The Kind Of Person You Want To Be Friends With

I want you to consider exactly how you exist in the world. I have always struggled to find mentors so I looked to my friends and they showed up. You need to start doing the same.

Be the mentor you wish you had when you needed help.

Be the friend you wish you had when you were alone.

Be the leader you wish you had when you needed someone to follow.

Be the partner you wish you had when you were building something meaningful.

Be the colleague you wish you had when you needed someone to cover your shift.

Be better to the people around you because you never know what they’re going through and you never know what they can bring into your life too.
