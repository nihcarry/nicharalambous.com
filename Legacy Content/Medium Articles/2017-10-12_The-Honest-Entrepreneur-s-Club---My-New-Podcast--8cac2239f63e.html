The Honest Entrepreneur’s Club — My New Podcast!

For the past year I’ve been fascinated by podcasts. My favourite is the How I Built This series (if you haven’t listened, make it a priority).

I’ve been hesitant to get started because I always want each bit of audio I put together to be perfect. This prevents me from actually publishing anything. I don’t feel the same way about my writing because I’ve been writing for so long that it feels so natural to me, like an extension of my brain.

Then I was introduced to Anchor.fm by Rich Mulholland (check out his podcast here) and decided to dive in. Anchor makes it simple to record, publish and list your podcast everywhere.

So here’s the announcement that I have a mini-podcast live on Anchor.fm. You can either head directly to my Achor.fm channel. Download the app and find me or you can find me on Apple Podcasts or Google Music Podcasts.

For now, the podcast is titled: The Honest Entrepreneur’s Club. This may change as I figure out what I end up focusing on but for now I’m talking as honestly and openly about my experience as an entrepreneur.

Give it a listen, give me your feedback, dial in and leave your comments!
