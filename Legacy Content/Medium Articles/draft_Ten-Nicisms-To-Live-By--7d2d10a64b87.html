Ten Nicisms To Live By. The ten things I believe that make me happier every day. The World View That Keeps Me Sane.

I have spent the better part of the last ten years trying to build a world view that helps me maintain a consistent level of happiness. 

This is the first article in a series that explains my world view, how I came to each rule and the detail surrounding each one. 

  1. There is nothing after this
  2. Learn the rules but ignore them
  3. Be honest if possible
  4. Be more
  5. Be patient
  6. Trust people until you have a reason not to
  7. Listen more, talk less
  8. Everything is easier without ego
  9. Strong opinions loosely held
  10. Burn it all down if you have to
  11. BONUS: Ask More Questions
